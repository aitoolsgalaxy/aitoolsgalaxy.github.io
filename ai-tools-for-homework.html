How to use AI for homework

Use AI as support for understanding and improving homework, not as a replacement for your own learning.

1

Ask for explanations first

Use AI tools to understand the question, topic, or assignment before trying to write the answer.

2

Improve your draft

Use tools like Grammarly or QuillBot to clean up grammar, spelling, and sentence clarity after you write.

3

Check and learn

Always review what the AI gives you and use it to learn the topic better rather than copying blindly.
