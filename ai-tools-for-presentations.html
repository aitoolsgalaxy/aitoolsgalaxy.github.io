How to use AI for presentations

Use AI as support for planning and improving presentations, not as a replacement for understanding your topic.

1

Build the structure first

Use AI tools to turn your topic into a simple slide outline with clear sections and main points.

2

Make visuals simple

Use tools like Canva to create cleaner slides, posters, and layouts that are easy to understand quickly.

3

Keep text short and clear

Always edit your slide text so it stays simple, readable, and supports what you say rather than overwhelming the audience.
